Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

FlyBuddy is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the FlyBuddy mobile application.

If location-based features are used, your device location may be accessed only with your permission and solely for providing airport navigation and travel assistance functionality.

Subscriptions

FlyBuddy offers optional premium subscriptions processed through Apple's App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. Payments are handled by Apple.

Data Storage

Certain settings and preferences may be stored locally on your device to improve your experience.

Third-Party Services

FlyBuddy may display information obtained from third-party travel, airport, aviation, or government sources. Their respective privacy policies may apply when accessing their services.

Your Rights

You may stop using the application at any time and remove locally stored data by deleting the app from your device.
