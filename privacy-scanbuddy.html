ScanBuddy Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

ScanBuddy respects your privacy. The app is designed to process and manage your documents directly on your device.

Data Collection

ScanBuddy does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data with JSPixelcraft or third parties.

Document Processing

All scanned documents, PDF files, edits, and document organization features are processed locally on your device.

Optical Character Recognition (OCR)

OCR functionality is performed locally using technologies provided by Apple. No scanned documents, images, or recognized text are uploaded to external servers for OCR processing.

Analytics and Tracking

ScanBuddy does not use analytics services, advertising networks, tracking technologies, or user profiling systems.

Advertising

ScanBuddy does not display third-party advertisements.

In-App Purchases

Optional in-app purchases are processed exclusively by Apple through the App Store. JSPixelcraft does not receive or store payment information.

Third-Party Services

The app relies on standard Apple frameworks and services required for iOS functionality. Any information processed by Apple is governed by Apple's own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

ScanBuddy does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
